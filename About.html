About Theo

Theo Mathebula is a Mechanical Engineering graduate from the University of the Witwatersrand (Wits), where he earned his Bachelor of Science in Engineering (BSc Eng). With a strong foundation in engineering principles, Theo has developed a keen interest in mechanical system design, problem-solving, and innovation.Currently working as a Junior Mechanical Designer, Theo is gaining hands-on experience in the design and development of mechanical components and systems. He is involved in creating technical drawings, 3D modeling, and collaborating with engineering teams to deliver practical solutions that meet industry standards.In addition to his role as a Junior Mechanical Designer, Theo has contributed to the academic community at Wits as a Student Tutor. There, he supported undergraduate engineering students by facilitating tutorials, assisting with assessments, and explaining complex concepts to help students succeed in their coursework.Theo has also gained valuable industry experience during his vacation work as a Junior Mechanical Engineering Assistant. This role provided him with exposure to real-world engineering projects and allowed him to work alongside senior engineers, gaining insights into the practical application of theoretical knowledge.Passionate about the intersection of design and functionality, Theo is dedicated to further developing his skills and contributing to the mechanical engineering field. He is committed to solving real-world challenges and advancing his career through continuous learning and hands-on experience
